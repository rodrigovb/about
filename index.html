Eñe'ẽ: Automatic Speech Recognition System for Guarani language

2016

Guarani is a language spoken by around eight million people. In Paraguay, about 25% of the population speaks only Guaraní. With the increase in the number of devices that include speech-aided personal assistants, the need to develop applications with voice interfaces is constantly growing. Despite being a widely spoken language, there is currently no research on automatic speech recognition in Guaraní. In this context, we propose a system of speech recognition in Guarani denominated Eñe'ẽ.
